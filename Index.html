OVERVIEW
Vehicle Maintenance Manager is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data — simply and transparently.

This Privacy Policy applies to the Vehicle Maintenance Manager Android application ("the App") developed by the developer ("we", "us", "our"). By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use the App:

  • Google Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address and profile photo from Google's authentication service.
  • Profile Information: Mobile number, state, city and pincode that you provide during profile setup.
  • Vehicle Data: Vehicle type, maker, model and registration number that you add to the App.
  • Maintenance Records: Service descriptions, amounts, dates and service types that you log in the App.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Authenticate you via Google Sign-In
  • Display your vehicle and maintenance records within the App
  • Generate PDF backup reports on your request
  • Improve the App's functionality and user experience

We do not use your information for advertising or marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

All your vehicle and maintenance data is stored locally on your device using SQLite database. We do not upload your vehicle data to any server or cloud storage.

Google Sign-In authentication is handled by Google's secure OAuth2 system. We only store your Google UID, name and email locally on your device to identify your account within the App.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent or share your personal information with any third parties except:

  • Google Sign-In: Authentication is processed by Google LLC as per their privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or legal process.

5. PDF Backup

When you tap "Download Backup PDF", the App generates a PDF file containing your vehicle and maintenance data and saves it to your device's Downloads folder. This file stays on your device and is not sent anywhere automatically. You control what you do with this file.

6. Permissions

The App requests the following permissions:

  • Internet: Required for Google Sign-In authentication.
  • Write External Storage (Android 9 and below): Required to save PDF backup to Downloads folder.

No other permissions are requested or required.

7. Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time by:

  • Deleting individual vehicles or records within the App
  • Uninstalling the App — this removes all locally stored data permanently
  • Revoking Google Sign-In access from your Google Account settings at myaccount.google.com

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes by updating the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
